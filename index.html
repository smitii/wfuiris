IRIS: Integrating Research in Science aims to showcase the importance of interdisciplinary interactions during this day-long conference at Wake Forest University on Saturday, April 14, 2018.

This conference has three main components: keynote speeches by Dr. Olga Pierrakos and Dr. Sara Quandt, breakout sessions led by student researchers, and a career panel featuring both academic and industry professionals.

IRIS will take place in Greene Hall by Manchester Plaza.
