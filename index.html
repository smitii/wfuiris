Workshop on Agent-Based Modeling

The Division of Applied Mathematics and the Leftschetz Center for Dynamical Systems at Brown University will host a two-day workshop on agent-based modeling on March 21st-22nd, 2015.

The aim of this workshop is to introduce students and young researchers to agent-based modeling by having a small group of experts give research presentations and tutuorials as well as directly involving students through a "math slam" and poster presentations. To apply for the workshop and funding, please visit this MathPrograms link.

 

Lectures locations will be announced in the future.
